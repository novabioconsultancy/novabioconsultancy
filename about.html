Our Story & Mission

Founded in September 2025 by three visionary healthcare innovators, NovaBio Consultancy is transforming African healthcare through strategic innovation in Pharmaceuticals, MedTech, and Digital Health.

Our Mission

At NovaBio Consultancy, we believe the future of healthcare lies at the intersection of innovation and impact. Our mission is simple yet profound: to help transform bold healthcare ideas into real-world solutions that improve lives across Africa and beyond.

We stand at the forefront of biotech and healthcare innovation, working closely with our clients to drive progress and shape a healthier future for all. Through our expertise in Pharmaceuticals, MedTech, and Digital Health, we're not just consulting – we're partnering in the transformation of African healthcare.

Our Journey

From a shared vision in September 2025 to becoming a leading healthcare innovation consultancy in Africa.

September 2025

NovaBio Founded

Armustarz Magomere, Brenda Karwitha, and Ivan Onyunde come together with a shared vision to transform African healthcare through strategic innovation, founding NovaBio Consultancy in Nairobi, Kenya.

October 2025

First Client Partnership

NovaBio secures its first major client partnership with a leading African pharmaceutical company, launching a comprehensive drug development strategy for the East African market.

December 2025

Digital Health Innovation Hub

Launch of the Digital Health Innovation Hub, providing African healthcare startups with access to cutting-edge AI and digital health technologies, along with implementation support.

2026

Expanding Impact

NovaBio continues to expand its impact across Africa, working with healthcare innovators to bring life-changing solutions to patients and communities throughout the continent.

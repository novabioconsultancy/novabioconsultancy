Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with NovaBio and our healthcare innovation process.

How quickly can NovaBio start working on my project?

We can typically begin initial consultations within 48 hours and start active project work within 1-2 weeks, depending on the complexity and scope of your healthcare innovation needs.

Do you work with international clients outside Africa?

Yes, while our expertise is focused on African healthcare markets, we work with international clients looking to enter or understand African healthcare systems and opportunities.

What makes NovaBio different from other consultancies?

Our deep understanding of African healthcare systems, combined with our founders' specialized expertise in Pharmaceuticals, MedTech, and Digital Health, allows us to provide culturally relevant and practically implementable solutions.

Can you help with regulatory approval in multiple African countries?

Yes, we have expertise in regulatory pathways across East Africa, West Africa, and Southern Africa, including Kenya, Nigeria, South Africa, and regional economic communities.

What's the typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Strategic planning projects typically take 2-3 months, while comprehensive development programs can span 6-18 months with ongoing support.
